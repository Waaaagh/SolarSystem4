saturn is the sixth planet from the sun and the second largest one in the solar system. the planet is known as a gas giant with a diameter of 120,536km that is about 9 earths stringing across it. the planet has many beautiful rings revolving around it . Although Saturns average density is much less than that of earth its volume is 95 times greater the planet is made up mainly of hydrogen. saturn was first discovered back in anchient days of babylon and gained its name as many planets do from the roman gods saturn was named for the roman god Saturnus but it was also known by the Greeks as Cronus.

Saturns rings are the biggest and best known set of rings in our solar system. the rings are made up of 7 groups with more than 30 different rings and are composed of billions of icy particles ranging in size from tiny grains to meters. the icy particles are made almost entirely out of water with trace components of rocky material. the rings are not seeable by the naked eye and were first seen by a telescope in 1610 by Galileo Galilei though he did not get a very good view the first person to be able to distinguish them as rings was Christiaan Huygens in 1655.
